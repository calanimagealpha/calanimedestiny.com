Exhibitor FAQ

Where do I register for Anime Destiny 2019 Artist Alley?

Unfortunately, all spots have been filled for this year. However, you can sign up for our waitlist here, which will stay open until October 3rd!, which will stay open until October 3rd!

How do I check my spot on the waitlist?

You can check your spot here!

When will you guys announce spots for next year?

Check our facebook page and emails (email us at webmaster@calanimagealpha.com to get added to our email list)! We will send out an announcement a week in advance, but you can generally expect sign-ups to start in early June!

What are your policies on adult/18+ content?

As a convention that takes place on a public campus and is open to the public/family friendly, we do not allow the display of 18+ content in our dealer hall or artist alley. You can read more about our policies at Anime Destiny Convention Policy.

What is your policy on proxy sellers?

Proxy sellers are wholly allowed at this convention, so long as the artist whose work is being sold confirms this fact with us and the name of the proxy seller/person picking up badges in advance.

Can I add another table/chairs day of?

Beyond accommodations for disabilities and anything requested in advance, we cannot offer changes to chairs or tables day of due to the nature of our venue. Sorry!

Can I, as the dealer/artist, pick up badges for others at my table and assistants?

Whoever name is on the registration will be able to pick up badges for everyone at their table, to expedite the check in/set up process!

What time can I start setting up at/what time can I take my stuff down?

The convention doors open at 10AM, and we allow artists and dealers to come in at 8AM to begin setting up. Please be patient with us the earlier you arrive, as we are also just beginning to set up our registration software at that time! The convention closes officially at 8PM, and we generally stay in the space until 10PM for clean up. Please pack up as soon as possible so we can help out and get staff out in a timely manner! If you need to leave earlier than 8PM, that is acceptable as well!
