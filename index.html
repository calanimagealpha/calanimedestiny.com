Anime Destiny is UC Berkeley's own annual anime convention, hosted by Cal Animage Alpha. It will be held on at the Martin Luther King Jr. Student Union on the south side of campus. We hope to bring together local fans and to provide an enjoyable, cozy experience for our attendees through fun events, interesting panels, and awesome merchandise! Stay tuned for our 2019 Date!
