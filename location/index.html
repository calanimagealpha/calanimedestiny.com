LOCATION
Anime Destiny is located at the Martin Luther King Jr. Student Union on the University of California, Berkeley campus at 2475 Bancroft Way, Berkeley, CA.

PARKING
Much of the street parking in Berkeley is limited to 2 hours or requires a city permit. Some of these permit areas (such as on the north side of campus) do not apply on weekends so you may be able to find street parking for free. However, there are many parking garages near campus which either charge a flat rate for the day or an hourly rate which is capped at a certain point.

PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION
BART
Ride the Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART) to the Downtown Berkeley station. After you get off, you can either walk for about 10-15 minutes to campus. You can also ride the 51B AC Transit bus (towards Rockridge BART) for $2.10 and get off at the Durant Ave/Telegraph Ave stop, a block away from MLK.

F Bus
If you live in the San Francisco area, the F Bus, which is a Transbay AC transit, will take you directly to the University. You can get on at the San Francisco Transbay Terminal (north-south by Mission Street and Howard Street). The Transbay fare is $4.20 one way for adults, $2.10 for youth, seniors, and disabled. The F Bus will take you directly to the south side of campus. You can get off the bus at the Bancroft Way/Telegraph Ave stop, directly outside MLK.

The Durant Food Court
The Durant Food Court is the most famous food court on the South side of campus, and one of the most popular places to eat. It consists of more than 10 restaurants which include Italian, Thai, Japanese, Chinese, donuts, and more. A great place to find boba tea and something satisfying for everyone.
