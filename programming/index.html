Interested in hosting a panel of your very own? Apply here! We will send you a confirmation after you send in your information, but panelist selections will likely be a bit later based on information from our sponsors/guests. All panelists will receive a badge for every participating member of their panel. Sign ups will close as space is filled or by September 30th.

Guests
If you are interested in suggesting a guest (or yourself!) as a guest for Anime Destiny 2019, please contact us at webmaster@calanimagealpha.com with a description of the suggested guests’ accolades, needs from the con, and areas of expertise. We generally offer a badge for the guest plus a helper, a panel spot if so desired, a tabling spot in our exhibit hall, and gas costs/cost of local public transportation.
Cosplay Contest
Anime Destiny’s cosplay contest is a tried and true tradition! We offer three grand prizes, for Craftsmanship, Performance, and Best in Show, with the equivalent of $50 each in prizes for the victors! If you are interested in registering for our masquerade, please fill out the form below. Sign ups will close at 2pm, day of the con. Please see us in person to register if you sign up day-of. We have some brilliant cosplay judges, returning and new this year! Please stay tuned for their info and official announcements of them as guests!

Anime Destiny LIVE
In response to the amount of amazing musical acts we’ve had participate in Anime Destiny these past few years, Anime Destiny is planning its first ever live show, complete with a menagerie of different varieties of nerdy musical acts, dance groups, an MC, and more! If you would like to suggest performers or apply yourself, please send us an email to webmaster@calanimagealpha.com with some links of your performances, info about what you would want on our end, and general information about your act/group! Lineup will be posted here in October, guests are currently TBA!
