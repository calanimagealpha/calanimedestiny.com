Guests
If you are interested in suggesting a guest (or yourself!) as a guest for Anime Destiny 2019, please contact us at webmaster@calanimagealpha.com with a description of the suggested guests’ accolades, needs from the con, and areas of expertise. We generally offer a badge for the guest plus a helper, a panel spot if so desired, a tabling spot in our exhibit hall, and gas costs/cost of local public transportation.

Cosplay Judges

Carmilla Jo

Carmilla Jo is a Bay Area-based cosplayer, model, and UC Berkeley graduate. Growing up with a love for fantasy, animation, and the avant-garde, she has evolved as not just a model and cosplayer, but also as a stylist and creative producer, teaming up with creative individuals around the Bay Area, Los Angeles, and Taipei. By taking creative control in her works, she has yielded numerous publications, interviews, and magazine cover features. As an award-winning cosplayer, her costuming mainly takes inspiration from anime, manga, video games, history, corsetry, and nostalgic cartoons. She plans to continue improving in the craft of cosplay and costuming in the years to come, in addition to collaborating with fellow ambitious creatives for more elaborate and other-worldly photoshoots and projects.

Trina Van

Trina, known as Unyuu Cosplay, has over 10 years of cosplaying experience, an unmatched passion for sewing, and an addiction to recreating the most minute of details. She has competed and won many awards throughout the years for both craftsmanship and performance including Best in Shows & Best Master Craftsmanship. She has an educational background in both fashion and costume design, and she loves working with intricate embellishments and embroidery when creating cosplays. Her favorite series to cosplay from is CLAMP, especially her favorite character, Sakura. Outside of cosplaying, she enjoys designing and wearing her own apparel and historically inspired costumes. She also loves to perform on stage with her friends at events and conventions and has been active as an idol under the group name Yuzu et Citrus with her partner Meririn.

Dbnibbles

Destinee, also known as Dbnibbles Cosplay, is an East Bay cosplayer and artist with a passion for animation and video games who started her cosplay journey in 2010, making her costumes from old clothing she found in her closet. It wasn’t until 2014 when she finally decided to construct her own cosplays from scratch. From that point on, she has continued to improve her skills in cosplay construction through learning how to sew and how to construct props with foam. She loves to compete in cosplay competitions and encourages others to get into cosplay no matter who they are.

Panelists

Cosplay Wrestling Federation

Established in 2013, World Cosplay Wrestling was founded by former Wall Street trader Lashayda DuCree. Using her vast fortune, Lashayda hosts extravagant matches staring the world's most loved (and not so loved) characters as they square off to prove their dominance in becoming the next champion and for her own personal amusement. Leave your power level scanners at the door folks, these contestants are putting their pride on the line and it is up to you, the fans, to see who walks away with the prized championship belt.
Performers

Intermission Orchestra

The Intermission Orchestra at Berkeley is a student-run orchestra at the University of California, Berkeley that plays music from anime, video games, and films. Founded by Daniel Lee and Zemei Zeng in Fall 2017, they play original arrangements of both famous hits and hidden gems to bring music from these media to a wider audience and spark a deeper appreciation for the pieces that may go unnoticed.
