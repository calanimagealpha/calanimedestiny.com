Register above for Anime Destiny 2019! Tickets are priced at $8 for the pre-registration period, ending the weekend before the con, and will be sold for $10 at the door! Please follow the appropriate signage day of , and bring either your ticket confirmation from Square or your valid photo ID to pick up your badge. Guests who register day of will have the option of card payment through Square, a PayPal charge to our club page, or cash.
